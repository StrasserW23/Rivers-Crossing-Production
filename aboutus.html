Meet the Team

Staff and leadership supporting worship and production at Rivers Crossing.

Our Story

Rivers Crossing Church was founded in 2007 by Pastor Paul Taylor and his wife Farrah in the Cincinnati area. What began as a small gathering of families has grown into a thriving church community serving thousands of people locally and online.

Each weekend our teams support multiple worship services and a live broadcast experience. Production plays a key role in creating an environment where people can experience worship both in the room and online.

The production team works closely with worship leaders, musicians, and creative staff to bring services to life through audio, lighting, video, and broadcast systems.
