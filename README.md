Project Title:
Rivers Crossing Production Team Website

Creator:
William J Strasser

Project Description:
This website is the digital home for the Rivers Crossing Production Team, giving visitors an inside look at our work and our team. It includes pages to welcome visitors, introduce the staff, showcase current and past productions, and share photos and videos from our services and events. While staff members’ contact info is available, our dedicated volunteer team works behind the scenes and is acknowledged without sharing personal information. The site is designed to be engaging, informative, and visually appealing for anyone interested in live production and church media.

Target Audience:
Primary: Church members and attendees who want to learn about our production team.
Secondary: Prospective team members, volunteers, and anyone curious about live production or media at Rivers Crossing.

Project Goals:

Welcome visitors and explain what they can explore on the site.
Introduce staff members and provide a way to contact them via email.
Showcase current and past productions with images and videos.
Give an inside look at the creative and technical work behind our services.
Celebrate and acknowledge the volunteer team’s contributions.
